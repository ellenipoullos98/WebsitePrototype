About us

People helping people.

Our purpose is to support and empower those that are vulnerable, both individual and community. We aim to reduce suffering across Australia via utilising the power of humanity.

Our vision, human diginity, peace, safety and wellbeing for all.

How we help.

To meet our goals for 2020, we have set six strategy goals:

  1. An active humanitarian movement
  2. Resilience in disasters
  3. Championing peace
  4. Overcoming adversity
  5. A strong Red Cross
  6. Blood saving lives
